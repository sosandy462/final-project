Kids Eat Right Annual Nutrition Gala and Silent Auction 2014

Fundraiser
The SDSU Student Nutrition Organization hosted it's 2nd Annual Kids Eat Right Dinner Gala and Silent Auction at the historic Hall of Champions in San Diego's Balboa Park. The evening began with a silent auction and networking followed by dinner and presentations. We were honored to have Alyssa McClelland RD as our keynote speaker on the subject of the NFL Fuel up to Play 60 and its benefit for children. Over $3,000 was raised from the silent auction, which benefited the Kids Eat Right Campaign, an initiative of the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics.

I served as the event coordinator and secretary. I was in charge of scheduling gala committee meetings and seting agenda.
